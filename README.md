# 📊 Insurance Power BI Dashboard
## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Insurance Opportunity Performance and Revenue Analysis Dashboard** created using **Microsoft Power BI**.

The dashboard analyzes opportunity stages, revenue amount, top opportunities, policy products, employees, and meeting dates. It helps understand which opportunities and products generate the highest revenue.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze opportunity performance by stage  
- Track revenue amount across opportunities  
- Identify top-performing opportunities  
- Compare revenue by insurance product  
- Monitor open and total opportunities  
- Analyze employee-wise opportunities  
- Filter data by meeting year  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 💰 Stage by Revenue
This visual shows revenue generated at different opportunity stages.

- Qualify Opportunity has the highest revenue.
- Negotiate stage has lower revenue.
- Propose Solution has the lowest revenue.

### 🏆 Top Opportunities
The dashboard shows the highest revenue opportunities, including:

- Fire  
- EL-Group Mediclaim  
- DB-Mega Policy  
- CVP GMC  

### 📈 Top Policies
The dashboard compares policies based on revenue amount.

- BE-Mega Policy  
- CVP GMC  
- DB-Mega Policy  
- DB-Terrorism  
- EL-Group Mediclaim  

### 🧩 Opportunity by Product
The product visual shows revenue contribution from:

- Mediclaim  
- Constructions & Infrastructure  
- Engineering  
- Financial Lines  
- Marine Hull  
- Marine Cargo  

### 👥 Opportunity Summary
The dashboard includes:

- Open Opportunities  
- Total Opportunities  
- Employee-wise opportunity analysis  
- Meeting year filter
